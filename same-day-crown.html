What is a same-day crown?

A crown caps and protects a damaged or weakened tooth. With CEREC digital technology, it's designed and milled right in the clinic — completing your treatment in a same day visit.

Same-day vs traditional crowns

Both give you a strong, natural-looking crown — the difference is how you get there.

Same-day CEREC crown

  • Completed on the same day
  • No temporary crown needed
  • Digital 3D scan — no putty impressions
  • Milled in the clinic while you wait
  • Usually one round of numbing

Traditional lab crown

  • Two visits, spread over weeks
  • Temporary crown in between
  • Made at an outside dental lab
  • Second visit to fit the crown
  • Often two rounds of numbing

Caring for your new crown

A same-day crown is easy to look after — treat it like a natural tooth.

  • Brush and floss daily, just like your natural teeth.
  • Avoid biting very hard objects like ice or pens to protect the ceramic.
  • Wear a nightguard if you grind your teeth at night.
  • Keep up regular dental check-ups and cleanings.
A CEREC crown can't decay, but the natural tooth underneath still can — good hygiene keeps it healthy for years.
